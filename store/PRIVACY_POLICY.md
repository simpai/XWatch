# XMate Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-19

XMate stores user-entered notes for X profiles.

## Data We Process
- Profile identifiers (user handle and/or user ID) used as keys.
- Notes you manually enter.
- Timestamps for created/updated records.

## Where Data Is Stored
- Data is stored locally in your browser profile and synchronized via `chrome.storage.sync` (or equivalent browser sync storage) when sync is enabled.
- We do not operate a separate external server for this extension.

## Data Sharing
- We do not sell or transfer your notes to third parties.
- Data may be synced by your browser vendor account according to that vendor's sync policy.

## Data Deletion
- You can delete notes inside the extension UI.
- Removing the extension and clearing sync data removes stored records according to browser sync behavior.

## Contact
- Set your contact email in store listing.
